About me

{% include image.html url="/images/mihir.jpg" width=180 align="right" %}

I am a postdoctoral researcher, presently working with Arash Nikoubashman at University of Mainz, Germany. I have previously worked with Glenn Fredrickson at Materials Research Laboratory in U. C. Santa Barbara. I obtained my Ph.D. at Cornell University, under the supervision of Fernando Escobedo.

I am broadly interested in structure-property relationships of soft materials, including polymers, colloids and nanoparticles. I aim to understand these relationships via theory and computer simulations, primarily using statistical mechanics.
